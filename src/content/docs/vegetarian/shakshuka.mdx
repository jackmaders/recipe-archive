## Ingredients

- **Olive oil** (30ml)
- **Cumin seeds** (2 tsp)
- **Red onion, sliced** (1 onion)
- **Yellow pepper, sliced** (1 pepper)
- **Chopped tomatoes** (400g)
- **Eggs** (2 eggs)

## Instructions

<Steps> 
1. Toast the cumin seeds in a small dry pan, over a medium heat.
2. Once the seeds start to pop, add a splash of olive oil and the sliced onion.
3. Once the onion starts to soften, add the sliced pepper and chopped tomatoes.
4. Season generously and simmer until the sauce thickens.
5. Make a small crater in the tomato mixture using the back of a spoon and crack the egg into it.
6. Place a lid on the pan and continue to simmer gently
7. Once the white is cooked but the yolk is still runny, remove from the heat and serve.

</Steps> 
:::tip[Serving Suggestions]

- As part of a tapas-style meal
- On it's own with sourdough or white bread

:::
